About Orca Falls

Orca Falls is Phillip Benjamin. They are a musician from Pottstown, a small town in Pennsylvania. Phil first learned to play guitar, followed by bass and keyboards.

Orca Falls is the embodiment of everything Phil grew up listening to. From Pink Floyd, to Radiohead, to Tame Impala, Orca Falls is a giant conglomerate of all the music Phil loves.

Although notably not British, Orca Falls takes influence from largely British and Australian bands, resulting in a European-sounding American artist. Ranging from classic metal overtones to spacey, psychedelic atmospheres, Orca Falls brings a fresh taste to modern rock.
